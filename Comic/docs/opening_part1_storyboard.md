# Barcodia — Chapter 1 Opening Storyboard

## Opening Title
**You’re Late Again**

## Scope
From the symbolic nightmare through the first small street skirmish. Stop immediately after the first fight resolves.

## Reading format
Left-to-right comic page prototype, manga/JRPG-inspired visual language.

## Page Breakdown

### Page 01 — Symbolic Nightmare
**Goal:** Create unease and foreshadow emotional loss without spoiling the sister tragedy or corruption arc.
**Tone:** fragmented, rainy, dreamlike, quiet

**Reference constraints:**
- hero_main_unnamed.png for face/body consistency only; do not reveal sister clearly

**Panels:**
- **Panel 1 (Large wide / Dream-space / rain / reaching):** The hero reaches through rain toward a distant, blurred figure. No clear face. No explicit death.
- **Panel 2 (Tall narrow / Almost-touching hands):** Two hands almost meet, but the space between them stretches unnaturally.
- **Panel 3 (Medium / Distorted symbols / ripples):** Water ripples, a strange symbol bends, and fragments of a realm flash by.
- **Panel 4 (Wide final / Empty dream-space):** The figure is gone. Only rain and silence remain.
  - Dialogue/SFX: optional whisper: “…wait…”

### Page 02 — Warm Bedroom Wake-Up
**Goal:** Contrast the nightmare with safety, sunlight, and a cozy lived-in room.
**Tone:** warm, sleepy, safe

**Reference constraints:**
- hero_bedroom_reference.png
- wakeup_1022_mood_reference.png
- hero_main_unnamed.png

**Panels:**
- **Panel 1 (Large establishing / Bedroom / morning sunlight):** Use the bedroom reference. Curtains move softly. Barcodia monitor/logo visible if desired.
- **Panel 2 (Medium / Hero asleep):** Hero is messy, sprawled, relatable, not heroic yet.
- **Panel 3 (Close-up / Clock / phone):** Show 10:22 clearly.
  - Dialogue/SFX: phone chime: ding
- **Panel 4 (Close-up / Hero eyes opening):** He is groggy, still emotionally carrying the dream.
  - Dialogue/SFX: Hero: “…mm?”
- **Panel 5 (Small / Phone in hand):** Notification glow leads into sister texts.

### Page 03 — Sister Texts / Realization
**Goal:** Establish the twin sister’s care, teasing, and concern while triggering the late-realization beat.
**Tone:** warm concern shifting to urgency

**Reference constraints:**
- twin_sister_blonde.png for message icon/avatar only
- hero_main_unnamed.png

**Panels:**
- **Panel 1 (Phone close-up / Text message 1):** Message bubble reads: “You awake?”
- **Panel 2 (Over-shoulder / Hero squints):** Hero tries to process the message half-awake.
- **Panel 3 (Phone close-up / Text message 2):** Message bubble reads: “Please tell me you’re not sleeping 😑”
- **Panel 4 (Phone close-up / Text message 3):** Message bubble reads: “You promised.”
- **Panel 5 (Phone close-up / Text message 4):** Message bubble reads: “…You okay?”
- **Panel 6 (Medium reaction / Hero fully realizes):** His eyes sharpen. The warmth flips into panic.
  - Dialogue/SFX: Hero: “…oh no.” / “I’m definitely late.”

### Page 04 — Scramble
**Goal:** Make the reader feel immediate momentum without turning him into a cartoon.
**Tone:** urgent, grounded, lightly comedic

**Reference constraints:**
- hero_main_unnamed.png
- hero_bedroom_reference.png

**Panels:**
- **Panel 1 (Medium / Bolts upright):** Hero sits up fast, blanket flying slightly.
  - Dialogue/SFX: Hero: “No, no, no—”
- **Panel 2 (Wide action / Blanket thrown aside):** He reaches for clothes while stumbling out of bed.
- **Panel 3 (Small action / Pulling on shirt):** Half-dressed rush, messy hair, one sock missing energy.
  - Dialogue/SFX: Hero: “I’m late.”
- **Panel 4 (Small / Grabs essentials):** Phone, money/wallet/pouch, shoes. Important setup for theft.
- **Panel 5 (Tall vertical / Door exit):** He rushes out the bedroom door.

### Page 05 — Mom Kitchen Moment
**Goal:** Add lived-in family warmth before the outdoor conflict begins.
**Tone:** warm, familiar, teasing

**Reference constraints:**
- kitchen_mother_reference.png
- mother.png
- hero_main_unnamed.png

**Panels:**
- **Panel 1 (Establishing / Kitchen):** Use kitchen reference. Mother cooking/stirring, sunlight, cozy lived-in home.
- **Panel 2 (Medium / Hero rushes in):** Hero crosses frame fast, still fixing clothes/hair.
- **Panel 3 (Medium close / Mother knowing smile):** She is not angry. This has happened before.
  - Dialogue/SFX: Mother: “Late again?”
- **Panel 4 (Wide exit / Hero rushing out):** Hero answers while already moving toward the door.
  - Dialogue/SFX: Hero: “I know… I know!”

### Page 06 — Running Through Town
**Goal:** Show that he really cares and establish the Barcodia village environment.
**Tone:** bright, urgent, JRPG town energy

**Reference constraints:**
- village_reference_from_Hero3.png
- hero_main_unnamed.png

**Panels:**
- **Panel 1 (Wide town / Village street):** Hero runs through the village. Use official village environment reference.
- **Panel 2 (Medium movement / Weaving through townspeople):** Street life: banners, trees, rooftops, people stepping aside.
- **Panel 3 (Close-up / Worried glance/phone):** He checks the time or glances ahead, worried about his sister waiting.
  - Dialogue/SFX: Hero thought: “Please don’t still be waiting…”
- **Panel 4 (Wide push / Determined run):** He leans forward and keeps running. He is trying.

### Page 07 — Street Con Begins
**Goal:** Show his kindness as a real character trait that costs him time.
**Tone:** urgent interrupted by concern

**Reference constraints:**
- hero_main_unnamed.png
- village_reference_from_Hero3.png

**Panels:**
- **Panel 1 (Medium action / Hero running):** Hero is moving fast through town.
- **Panel 2 (Small interruption / Child calls out):** A small child suddenly appears distressed at the side of the street.
  - Dialogue/SFX: Child: “Mister! Please—!”
- **Panel 3 (Medium child / Fake distress):** The child reaches toward him, anxious face. Keep it convincing.
  - Dialogue/SFX: Child: “Can you help me?!”
- **Panel 4 (Reaction / Hero stops):** Hero stops immediately despite being late.
- **Panel 5 (Close/low / Hero leans down):** He listens with genuine concern.
  - Dialogue/SFX: Hero: “Hey, what happened?”

### Page 08 — The Theft
**Goal:** Flip sympathy into surprise and launch the chase.
**Tone:** quick reversal

**Reference constraints:**
- hero_main_unnamed.png
- village_reference_from_Hero3.png

**Panels:**
- **Panel 1 (Tight detail / Snatch):** The child’s hand slips the money pouch/wallet from hero’s pocket or bag.
- **Panel 2 (Action / Kid bolts):** The child runs with the stolen item.
- **Panel 3 (Close-up / Hero realizes):** Hero’s face shifts from concern to disbelief.
  - Dialogue/SFX: Hero: “Hey—!”
- **Panel 4 (Wide chase / Hero pursues):** Hero lunges forward into the chase.
  - Dialogue/SFX: Hero: “Wait!”

### Page 09 — The Blockade
**Goal:** Transition from chase to first combat/tutorial fight.
**Tone:** street-level tension, not dark

**Reference constraints:**
- hero_main_unnamed.png
- village_reference_from_Hero3.png

**Panels:**
- **Panel 1 (Chase / Rounding corner):** Hero follows the child into a tighter street or side lane.
- **Panel 2 (Medium reveal / Older accomplices step out):** Two or three older street punks block his path.
- **Panel 3 (Close-up / Hero stops short):** He tenses, breathing hard, not backing down.
  - Dialogue/SFX: Hero: “Move.”
- **Panel 4 (Wide confrontation / Path blocked):** Thugs fill the street between him and the fleeing kid.
  - Dialogue/SFX: Thug: “Run along.” / Hero: “Give it back.”

### Page 10 — First Skirmish
**Goal:** Show the hero as determined, imperfect, scrappy, and kind-hearted under pressure.
**Tone:** grounded JRPG first fight

**Reference constraints:**
- hero_main_unnamed.png
- village_reference_from_Hero3.png

**Panels:**
- **Panel 1 (Action start / Thug lunges):** One thug shoves or swings first.
- **Panel 2 (Awkward defense / Hero blocks late):** Hero defends imperfectly. He is not a flawless fighter.
  - Dialogue/SFX: Hero: “—!”
- **Panel 3 (Counter / Hero pushes back):** Hero uses momentum to shove/counter.
  - Dialogue/SFX: Hero: “I said—”
- **Panel 4 (Impact / Grounded hit/stumble):** A believable hit or trip. No excessive violence.
  - Dialogue/SFX: SFX: THUD
- **Panel 5 (Wide resolution / Accomplices stagger):** The small criminals are stunned, retreating, or down enough for the scene to resolve.
  - Dialogue/SFX: Hero: “Give it back.”
- **Panel 6 (End beat / Hero breathing hard):** Hero stands breathing hard after winning the first small exchange. STOP HERE.
