# Canon Reference Lock — Barcodia Opening

These are the locked reference rules for this prototype.

## Characters

- `references/canon_characters/hero_main_unnamed.png` is the main hero for this opening. He does not have a confirmed name yet.
- `references/canon_characters/twin_sister_blonde.png` is the official twin sister design for this opening.
- `references/canon_characters/mother.png` is the mother design.
- `references/canon_characters/maverick_antihero_not_protagonist.png` is Maverick, an anti-hero main character, but he is **not** the protagonist and should not appear in this opening unless later approved.

## Environments

- `references/environments/hero_bedroom_reference.png` is the bedroom reference.
- `references/environments/kitchen_mother_reference.png` is the kitchen reference.
- `references/environments/village_reference_from_Hero3.png` is the village reference.
- `references/environments/wakeup_1022_mood_reference.png` is the wake-up mood/time reference. Use 10:22 as the late wake-up time.

## Style Rule

The intended finish is smooth, clean, anime/JRPG-inspired comic art. Avoid rough, grainy, or overly sketchy rendering.

## Design Rule

Do not invent new character designs, costumes, or major environment designs without explicit approval.
