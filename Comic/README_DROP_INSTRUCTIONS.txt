BARCODIA DROP-IN UPDATE — PAGES 010–017

This zip contains:
- page-010.png through page-017.png
- an updated js/chapter-data.js that tells the reader there are now 17 pages

How to install:
1. Unzip this file.
2. Copy/drag the included `chapters` folder and `js` folder into the root of your current Barcodia comic reader project.
3. Allow it to merge/overwrite files.
4. Refresh index.html in your browser.

Important:
The reader will NOT automatically detect extra page PNGs unless chapter-data.js includes them.
That is why this zip includes the updated chapter-data.js.

This is still rough storyboard art. Dialogue/panel fixes can be handled in a later polish pass.
